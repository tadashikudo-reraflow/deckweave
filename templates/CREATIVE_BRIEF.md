# Creative Brief

## Purpose

What this deck must achieve.

## Audience

Who will read or watch it, and what they already believe or need.

## Desired Action

What the audience should do after seeing the deck.

## Core Message

The one idea the deck must make memorable.

## Product / Proposal Appeal

The real advantage, evidence, or emotional hook.

## Context

Where and how this deck will be used.

## Avoid

Unrelated visuals, generic AI decoration, explanation-only layouts, and claims
that are not supported by the source material.
